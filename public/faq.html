The Process

You fill out the contact form. I follow up within 24 hours. Then I build a real, working, clickable preview of your site — no payment yet. You review it, tell me what needs changing, and we go back and forth until it's perfect. Once you approve, All Spark Enterprises Ltd. sends an invoice via Square. You pay, the site goes live on Cloudflare.
No. Not a dollar. You see the working site before any invoice is ever sent. That's the whole model — build first, pay after you approve.
Reasonable revisions within the original scope are included — tweaking wording, colors, layout, sections. If you want to add whole new pages or major features that weren't in the original brief, that's out-of-scope work billed at an hourly rate. I'll always be upfront before I touch anything extra.
A simple site can be ready for your review in a few days. More complex multi-page builds take longer. I'll give you a realistic timeline after we talk through your scope.

Pricing & Payment

Every project is scoped and priced specifically for what you need — no packages to cram yourself into. Check the portfolio for a sense of past project pricing. Fill out the contact form for a straight quote.
No monthly fees from me. You pay for the build once. The only ongoing costs are your domain (typically $15–20/year from a registrar) and hosting (Cloudflare Pages has a generous free tier). I'll walk you through all of it.
Invoices are issued by All Spark Enterprises Ltd. — the Ontario corporation that Local Launch Sites operates under — and processed through Square. You pay by card and receive a clean receipt. All prices are in Canadian dollars plus 13% HST.
Anything beyond the agreed scope is billed at my hourly rate. I'll always tell you before I start, get your go-ahead, and only invoice for actual time spent. No surprises.

Technical Details

Primarily static HTML/CSS/JS — clean, fast, and built to last. No WordPress, no page builders, no subscription platforms. Your site loads extremely fast and doesn't need constant security updates or plugin management. For businesses that genuinely need a CMS or e-commerce, we can discuss — but most small local businesses don't.
Sites are deployed on Cloudflare Pages — one of the fastest and most reliable platforms available, with a free tier that covers most small business sites. Your site is served from Cloudflare's global network, meaning fast load times for visitors everywhere.
Every single site is built fully responsive and mobile-first. It'll look sharp on any phone, tablet, or desktop — no exceptions.
Yes — your domain is registered separately through a domain registrar. I'll help you pick one and connect it to your site. Most .ca domains run $15–25/year.
Sites are built in plain HTML — technically editable if you know HTML. For most owners, the easier answer is to contact me. Small updates and text changes are quick and billed at the hourly rate.

Think of it like a division that does one specific thing really well. All invoices and payments are handled by All Spark Enterprises Ltd.
