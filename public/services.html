How It Works

From "Yes" to Live

A straightforward process with no surprises — designed to respect your time.

01
Day 1

You Tell Us About Your Business

Chat with our AI agent or fill out a short form. We need to know what you do, who your customers are, and what a great website would look like for you. Takes about 10 minutes.

02
Day 1–2

You Choose a Package & Pay the Deposit

Pick Starter, Standard, or Premium. Pay securely via Square. We don't start building until the deposit is in — that keeps things clean and fair for both sides.

03
Day 2–6

We Build It

Hand-coded, no templates, no page builders. We write the copy, design the layout, configure the AI agent, and set up everything on a staging URL for you to review.

04
Review

You Review the Preview

We send you a live staging link. Look it over, make notes, send us your changes. One round of revisions is included. Most clients need minor tweaks — we handle them same day.

05
Launch

Your Site Goes Live

We connect your domain, flip the switch, and submit to Google Search Console. You own everything — the domain, the code, the hosting account. We just build it.
