Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

All Spark Enterprises Ltd. operates Local Launch Sites from Devlin, Ontario. References to "we," "us," or "our" mean All Spark Enterprises Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian law.

What We Collect

When you submit the project intake form, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business name, address, city, and province
  • Project details — pages needed, features, budget range, timeline, and style preferences
  • Any files you upload (logos, reference images, photos)
  • Whether you have an existing website and its URL

We do not collect payment card information through this site. All payments are processed through Square, subject to Square's own privacy and security policies.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to respond to your inquiry, prepare and deliver your Scope of Work, build and deliver your website, invoice you through All Spark Enterprises Ltd., and communicate about the project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

AI Processing

Information submitted through the intake form is processed using Google Gemini (via the Gemini API) to automatically generate a Scope of Work document tailored to your project. This processing is used only to produce your project proposal and is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your project. Google's data use policies apply to data sent to the Gemini API.

How Data Is Stored

Form submissions are sent to a Google Apps Script endpoint and stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to All Spark Enterprises Ltd. Uploaded files (logos, photos) are saved to a private Google Drive folder. All data is stored in Google's infrastructure and subject to Google's privacy and security practices. We do not use third-party CRM platforms or marketing databases.

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels. We do not run advertising or retargeting campaigns. Google Fonts is loaded for typography — Google may log IP addresses of font requests. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Pages — website hosting. Cloudflare may log basic access data (IP address, request information) for CDN and security purposes.
  • Google Fonts — typography assets served from Google's servers.
  • Google Apps Script & Sheets — form submission processing and storage.
  • Google Drive — storage of uploaded files and project documents.
  • Google Gemini API — AI processing to generate your Scope of Work.
  • Square — payment processing for invoices issued by All Spark Enterprises Ltd.

Data Retention

We retain project inquiry and client data for a reasonable period consistent with our business and legal obligations under Ontario and Canadian law. Once a project is complete and the business relationship has ended, we may retain records as required for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable Ontario legislation, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, and to withdraw consent to its use at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us through the contact page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us through the contact page. All Spark Enterprises Ltd. operates Local Launch Sites from Devlin, Ontario.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the current version. Continued use of this site constitutes acceptance of any updates.
