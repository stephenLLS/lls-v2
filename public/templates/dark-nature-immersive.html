The Experience

Silence You Can Actually Hear

There's a reason people come back every year. Out here, a loon call echoes for a kilometre. The mist lifts off the water at 5am. The stars are so thick you can navigate by them.

Boreal Ridge Outfitters operates across 400,000 acres of licenced wilderness in the Temagami and Chapleau regions — two of Northern Ontario's last truly wild corners.

  • Float-plane access to remote base camps
  • All-season expeditions — spring through late fall
  • Groups of 2 to 12, fully catered or self-guided
  • Satellite phones and emergency protocols on every trip
  • Leave No Trace certified operations
