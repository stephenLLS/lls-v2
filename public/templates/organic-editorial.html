Our Story

Good food starts
with good soil.

We started Roots & Greens because we believed something simple: a meal made with honest ingredients — grown nearby, harvested in season, handled with care — tastes like nothing else. That belief became a kitchen, and that kitchen became a community.

Every morning, our team sources produce directly from farms within 100 km. What arrives at our door that day shapes the menu. Nothing is forced. Everything is fresh. We work with the land, not against it.

— Maya & Tom, Founders
