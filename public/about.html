Our Story

How This Started

Local Launch Sites grew out of a frustration we kept hearing from business owners across the region: web design was either too expensive, too slow, or too complicated. National agencies quoted thousands of dollars. DIY builders promised simplicity but delivered headaches. And nothing was built with Northwestern Ontario in mind.

We started small — a few local businesses, handshake deals, and a genuine belief that a great website could change things for a small business owner. And it did. Customers started finding them on Google. Questions that used to eat up phone time got handled by an AI agent at 2am. Businesses started looking as professional as they'd always been.

We're a division of All Spark Enterprises Ltd., a Fort Frances-based technology company with roots in the community and a long-term commitment to the region. Local Launch Sites is our answer to the question every small business owner deserves to ask: "Can I have a great website too?"

Yes. You can. And it shouldn't cost a fortune to get one.
